## Current / Most Recent Role
**Senior UX Designer — Invoca** (Apr 2022 – Mar 2026, 3 yrs 11 mos)
AI-powered conversation intelligence platform, B2B SaaS. Designed core contact center workflows and AI-native product experiences.

**Contact Center Call Review Redesign:**
- Led end-to-end research and redesign of the core call review workflow — primary daily interface for contact center QA teams
- Drove product growth from ~$4–6M to $12–15M ARR (~3x). CS team directly linked CRC adoption to reduced churn risk
- Ran a multi-phase research program: FullStory session analysis across 8 accounts, 58 individual call reviews, interviews with 5 companies/8 users, competitive analysis (Kayak, Carvana, Gong, Observe.ai)
- Research surfaced a data trust crisis (accuracy rated 45–50%), workflow inefficiency (22-min sessions reviewing calls in under 2 mins each), and structural UX failures
- Adopted a filter-first interaction model as the product's core navigation pattern, replacing legacy table-first layout
- Designed the human-AI trust model: AI performs initial scoring, reviewers maintain override authority
- Established reusable component patterns for transcript interaction, commenting, and scoring — became standards across subsequent product areas
- Established UX Review process (design reviews in-progress engineering work before QA) — overcame resistance to make it standard practice

**AI Signal Design & Model Training:**
- Designed interfaces for multiple AI signal types: keyword-based signals, LLM-powered semantic signals, and custom-trained model signals built from annotated call data
- Led UX concept for a self-service model training workflow to reduce new customer onboarding from 8+ hours of analyst support to under 1 hour of guided self-service
- Designed four-stage workflow: Topic Explorer (auto-surface themes from call data), Signal Blueprint (structured signal definition with candidacy checks), LLM-as-Judge training (dual-AI evaluation, humans arbitrate disagreements only), Automated Validation (accuracy assessment replacing manual spreadsheet testing)
- Built interactive Figma Make prototype for stakeholder communication and end-to-end concept validation
- Identified "bookends problem": front door (no use case guidance) and back door (no automated validation) were both broken
